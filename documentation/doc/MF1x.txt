 [I] 	We_do_mass_flows, 0=no, 1=bingham flow, 2= glen's law flow 

This 3-valued switch determines whether mass flow occurs and which
rheology model to use:

0:  Mass flows are not modeled
1:  Mass flow utilizes Bingham rheology
2:  Mass flow utilizes Glen's law rheology

See poput MF0x for more details
