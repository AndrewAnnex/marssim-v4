  SLOPE_DIFFUSIVITY  

This is the diffusivity for all sub-models of regolith mantled
slope mass wasting. Slope diffusivity varies widely among different
landscape, ranging from 0.0002 m**2/yr to perhaps 0.06 in solifluction
terrain.  Fairly typical values range from 0.004 to 0.012.

See MW0x popup for more details.
