 WEATHER_DECAY_RATE WITH DEPTH BENEATH SURFACE 

This governs how rapidly weathering decays with regolith depth.
weathering decays as a negative exponential function of regolith
depth.  With regolith thickness measured in meters, a 
weather_decay_rate of 2.0 to 3.0 has been measured. 

See popup WE0x for more details.
